👕 Uniform Guide

Class A Uniform (Formal)

  • Khaki shirt with BSP patch on left pocket
  • Green pants/skirt
  • Neckerchief with slide
  • Scout belt
  • Rank badge on left sleeve
  • Outfit number patch on right sleeve

Class B Uniform (Activity)

  • BSP t-shirt (green or white)
  • Maong pants or scout pants
  • Scout cap (optional)

Badge Placement Guide:

Left Pocket: BSP World Crest, Rank Badge
Right Pocket: Temporary patches, event badges
Left Sleeve: Council patch, patrol emblem
Right Sleeve: Outfit number, position patch
