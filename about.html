About Outfit 306

Honoring tradition, building character, serving the community

What is Boy Scouts of the Philippines?

The Boy Scouts of the Philippines (BSP) is the national scouting organization of the Philippines. Founded in 1936, it aims to develop young people physically, mentally, and morally through the Scout Oath and Law.

BSP is a member of the World Organization of the Scout Movement and has produced generations of leaders, public servants, and outstanding citizens.

History of Outfit 306

Outfit 306 was established at Dueñas General Comprehensive High School in 1995. Since then, it has grown into one of the most active scouting units in the region, producing numerous Eagle Scouts and community leaders.

Our outfit has participated in regional jamborees, community service initiatives, and leadership training programs, consistently earning recognition for excellence.

Our Foundation

Mission & Vision

Mission

To develop morally straight, disciplined, and community-oriented young leaders through the scouting movement and values-based education.

Vision

A nation of responsible, proactive, and God-fearing youth leaders who contribute meaningfully to society and the global community.
