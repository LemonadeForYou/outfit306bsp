About Outfit 306

Learn about our history, mission, and the Scouting spirit

What is the Boy Scouts of the Philippines?

The Boy Scouts of the Philippines (BSP) is the national Scouting organization of the Philippines. Founded in 1936, it was granted a charter by the Philippine National Assembly. BSP is a member of the World Organization of the Scout Movement (WOSM) and has been shaping Filipino youth into responsible citizens for over 85 years.

Scouting in the Philippines instills values of patriotism, leadership, and community service. The organization follows the Scout Oath and Law as its guiding principles, helping young people develop character, citizenship, and personal fitness.

History of Outfit 306

Outfit 306 was established at Dueñas General Comprehensive High School to provide students with opportunities for character development and leadership training. Named as the 306th registered outfit in the region, this troop has become a cornerstone of student development in Dueñas, Iloilo.

For over a decade, Outfit 306 has produced numerous Eagle Scouts, participated in district and regional jamborees, and led community service projects that have made a lasting impact on Barangay Dueñas and surrounding communities.

Our scouts are known for their discipline, dedication, and the "Laging Handa" (Always Ready) spirit that defines true Scouts of the Philippines.

📜 Our Mission

To develop morally straight, disciplined, and self-reliant youth through the Scout Oath and Law, preparing them for responsible leadership in school, community, and nation.

🌟 Our Vision

To be the premier organization in Dueñas General Comprehensive High School that molds future leaders dedicated to God, country, and community, producing Eagle Scouts who exemplify excellence and service.

The Scout Law

1. Trustworthy

A Scout's honor is to be trusted.

2. Loyal

A Scout is loyal to his country, leaders, and friends.

3. Helpful

A Scout is helpful and concerned about others.

4. Friendly

A Scout is a friend to all and a brother to every other Scout.

5. Courteous

A Scout is polite and respectful.

6. Kind

A Scout is kind to animals and understands nature.

7. Obedient

A Scout follows the rules of his family, school, and troop.

8. Cheerful

A Scout looks for the bright side of life.

9. Thrifty

A Scout saves and respects property.

10. Brave

A Scout faces danger with courage.

11. Clean

A Scout keeps his body and mind clean.

12. Reverent

A Scout respects his faith and is faithful to his beliefs.
